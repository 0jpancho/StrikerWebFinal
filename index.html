About Us

SPHS Robotics operates out of Saint Patrick High School, an all-boys Catholic school out of Chicago, Illinois. We host two FTC teams, 10240 and 10481, and one FRC team, 101 STRIKER. We strive to come together as a team in order to accomplish the task at hand, no matter the challenge.
